Check The NRA Website!

The NRA or the Nvelliana Royal Army is a Homebrew or a Fan-made Imperial Guard Regiment for Warhammer 40k. Their docrine and equipment closely resembles those of the past armies of Terra during the 21st Century. The NRA, along with the Cadian Shock Troop Regiments are the only few fighting units within the Imperium that still retain the ability to maintain their Combined Arms Regiments post Horus Heresy.
